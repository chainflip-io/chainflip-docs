## Chainflip Networks

We currently support different networks for different purposes:

- **Mainnet**: Our mainnet deployment. Learn more in [Mainnet Documentation](../../mainnet/validator-setup/prerequisites).
- **Perseverance**: Our persistent testnet. Learn more in [Perseverance Documentation](../../perseverance/validator-documentation).
- **Sisyphos**: Our staging network where we test releases before upgrading Perseverance.
